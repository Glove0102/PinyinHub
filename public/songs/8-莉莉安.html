莉莉安

She discovered the lonely person

Preparing to set off

So she prayed at dusk

Until night

She turned her head and heard

The sorrowful sobbing

A kind-hearted woman

With long hair hanging over her shoulders

She already followed the dusk

Arriving

The emerald green clothes in the furnace

Turned to ashes

Rising flames

Burning until dawn

Until

That woman pushed the door open and left

She talked to herself

In a place far away from here

There is a beach

The lonely person is just at sea

Holding the sail

If you see him return to the shore

Please tell him your name
